BRYCE STRADLING

About

Bryce Stradling is a graphic designer, developer, artist and musician living in Omaha, Nebraska. He received his degree in Graphic Design from the Creative Center, a professional graphic design school, and has extensive freelance experience in visual design, interaction design, illustration and animation for a variety of clients. In addition to his design work, Bryce enjoys writing and recording music, biking, writing and studying philosophy.
